My 6 Go to Tips

  1. Nutritional water/H2O flavor: Lemon wedge, or in my case a lime wedge in a glass of water.
  2. Cold prevention: Two or three tablespoons of ACV specifically Bragg's Apple Cider Vinegar with the mother-has all the health benefits. Another time would be after eating too many sweets.
  3. For a cold:ACV mixed with Emergen-C. I prepare the concoction and drink it at least twice a day, morning and evening in a 16oz water bottle.
  4. Allergy relief:a tablespoon or so of local honey.
  5. Cuts or burn/my cats random patches: Tea tree oil(half a drop when applying to animal!)
  6. An Apple a day! Carrying a water bottle with me everywhere I go.

*Disclaimer I am not a doctor or a trained professional. I am simply sharing my findings as to what has worked for me. Please consult with your doctor, or other health care professional before trying any of my tips.*
